Investment Focus

We invest where we can create value through operational improvements, repositioning, and disciplined execution. If you’re unsure whether a deal fits, send it anyway — we respond quickly.

Property types

Single-Family

Selective acquisitions in strong neighborhoods with durable demand drivers.

Small Multifamily

Value-add opportunities with clear renovation plans and rent growth rationale.

Off-Market

Direct-to-seller situations where speed and certainty matter.

Criteria (high level)

Category What we like What to include
Location Neighborhoods with stable demand and strong long-term fundamentals Address, submarket notes, comps if available
Condition Clear path to improvements (cosmetic to moderate rehab) Photos, scope notes, known issues
Income Room to improve NOI through operations, rents, or unit quality Rent roll, leases, utility info (multifamily)
Structure Straightforward terms, realistic timelines, clean closing process Asking price, preferred close date, access details

Deal submission

Email is fine. If you have a link to photos, a short doc, or a rent roll, include it.

  • Property address + asking price
  • Photos + condition notes
  • Rent roll (if applicable)
  • Your timeline + access details

Response time

We aim to respond quickly with either (1) interest + next steps or (2) a clear “not a fit.”
